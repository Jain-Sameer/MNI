About

MechNest Industries aims to develop broad based facilities for undertaking precision manufacturing of engineering parts. We have initiated with setting up of two Vertical Milling Centre Machines with a table size of 800 x 450 mm and 1000 x 500 mm. We also have a heavy duty standard manual lathe with a 12" centre & a 35A capacity EDM machine.

We are pleased to inform that we have already made complex Extrusion Dies (for solar aluminium sections) which are working successfully. These dies are being used by the largest solar aluminium section manufacturer in the country which is in turn supplying these sections to companies all over India.

We have developed Cutting Dies which are used in PCB manufacturing industries in addition to many parts and components which are used in locking and linkage mechanisms in plastic machineries and we are looking forward to adding many more engineeringÂ products.

Founder

Aayush Bansal is a B.Tech (Mechanical) graduate from Pandit Deendayal Energy University, Gandhinagar. Born in Patiala (Punjab), he was raised and did his schooling in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. He cultivated a passion for mechanical field while he was pursuing his undergraduate degree as he interned at Tata Motors (Sanand plant) and at Buhler India Pvt Ltd (Bangalore) which further enhanced his fascination with the manufacturing field and developed his entrepreneurial interest. He also holds one year of experience in Reliance Industries Limited (Jamnagar Manufacturing Division) as a Graduate Engineer Trainee.

Alongside his business inclinations, Aayush is passionate about various hobbies such as writing, watching movies, listening to music, playing video games, traveling, and engaging in sports.

In 2023, he left his job to launch his own start-up, MechNest Industries, which focuses primarily on manufacturing aluminum extrusion dies for the solar industry. His experience, skills, and enthusiasm make him confident in leading the company to success under his leadership.
