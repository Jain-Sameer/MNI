About

MechNest Industries has developed broad based facilities for undertaking precision manufacturing of engineering parts. We have set up facilities comprising two Vertical CNC Milling Centre Machines with a table size of 800 x 450 mm and 1000 x 500 mm besides having heavy duty lathe with 12" centre & a 35A capacity EDM machine.

We are specialized in making very complex Aluminum Extrusion Dies for solar panel aluminium sections which and have been appreciated for meeting the desired level of accuracy and output. We are the sole Indian vendor of these Extrusion Dies to the largest solar aluminium section manufacturer in India which is in turn supplies high quality sections to solar panel manufacturing companies across the country.

We have also designed and developed Cutting Dies which are used in high speed PCB manufacturing process.

We are also associated with a few reputed Indian machines manufacturers as one of their preferred vendor for producing industrial parts for them.

Founder

Aayush Bansal is a B Tech (Mechanical) graduate from Pandit Deendayal Energy University, Gandhinagar. He also holds one year of experience in Reliance Industries Limited (Jamnagar Manufacturing Division) as a Graduate Engineer Trainee.

During his engineering, he cultivated a passion for hard core manufacturing field while undertaking his summer training at Tata Motors (Sanand Plant) and later a full semester internship at Buhler India Pvt Ltd (Bangalore). Both these stints coupled with all encompassing and enriching training period in Reliance, he developed keen sense of design and manufacturing skills besides developing his entrepreneurial interest.

He launched his own engineering start-up, MechNest Industries, which focuses primarily on manufacturing aluminum extrusion dies for the solar industry. His experience, skills and enthusiasm make him confident in achieving benchmarks in the field.
